## Description (≤4000)
Gravity Dash is a one-thumb arcade game about a single, satisfying move: TAP to flip gravity.

Your runner never stops. Tap to fall up to the ceiling, tap again to drop to the floor. Time it right to slip past the walls, sweep up coins, and push your score higher as the speed ramps up. Miss once and it’s over — one more run, every time.

• ONE-TAP CONTROL — anyone can play in a second, nobody masters it
• ENDLESS & PROCEDURAL — every run is different, no two boards the same
• CHASE YOUR BEST — a score that climbs as fast as your nerve
• UNLOCK SKINS — spend coins on neon ball skins
• REVIVE & DOUBLE — keep the run alive or double your coins
• PURE FEEL — punchy haptics, particles and screen shake

No account. No timers. Just tap, flip, and dodge.

Free to play with optional ads and a one-time Remove Ads.
