# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 20 June 2026_

This privacy policy applies to the mobile games **Gravity Dash**, **Flap Dash**,
and **Jump Dash** (the "Games") developed by Furkan Ataman ("we", "us").

We designed the Games to be privacy‑friendly. **We do not ask you to create an
account and we do not collect personal information ourselves.** Your scores,
coins and settings are stored **only on your device**.

## Third‑party services

The Games use a small number of third‑party services that may process limited
data to function:

- **Google AdMob** (advertising). Shows ads and may use device identifiers and
  usage data to deliver and measure ads. On first launch the Games ask, via
  Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, whether you allow tracking — you may
  decline, and you will still see (non‑personalized) ads. You can remove ads
  entirely with the optional one‑time "Remove Ads" purchase.
  See Google's policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Apple Game Center** (leaderboards). If you are signed in to Game Center,
  your best score may be submitted to a leaderboard under your Game Center
  identity. This is optional and managed by Apple.
  See Apple's policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **Apple StoreKit** (purchases). Processes the optional "Remove Ads" purchase.
  We never see your payment details.

## Children

The Games are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect
personal information from children. Ads are delivered by Google AdMob.

## Data we store

- On‑device only: high scores, coins, selected skin, sound/haptic settings,
  and whether you purchased "Remove Ads". Deleting the app removes this data.

## Your choices

- Decline app tracking at the ATT prompt (or in iOS Settings ▸ Privacy ▸
  Tracking).
- Purchase "Remove Ads" to stop seeing ads.

## Changes

We may update this policy; the date above reflects the latest version.
